How to Play

Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the 'A' and 'S' keys to mine the block in the center, earning a point for each one you successfully whack. Each time you whack a block, the tower will shrink by 1. Be careful of the spiked branches above you though as they fall, if they smack you it will cause critical damage and trust me...
You don't want that!
Collect Blorgs to spend in the shop on some fancy new cosmetic items or game sounds! There are three modes to choose from:

  • Arcade

    You're injured but you still turned up for work? There's no saving you and you know it, but do your best to collect as many satte-logs as possible, 'kay champ? At least they provide a small amount of vitality so you won't meet your maker too quickly. Lets see just how long you can prolong the inevitable. Oh yeah, I almost forgot, there's a medium chance of finding Blorgs so grab as many as you can. I'll make sure your family get them.

  • Speed

    Welcome back astro-ranger. Your mission is to mine 100 logs as fast as possible. Try to beat your best time but remember there's a high chance of Blorgs appearing so you know what to do... unless you don't you want to be rich?

  • Endless

    It's the weekend but you've decided to work overtime? Okay, no problem. Kick back and take your time, just be careful not to leave more blood on the spikes hahaha! Oh yeah, there's less Blorgs around on the weekends so I hope you're not trying to save up or anything.
